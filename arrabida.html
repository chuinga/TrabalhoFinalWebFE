Setúbal

In my humble opinion, Setúbal has three major attractions: Arrábida, Beaches and gastronomy, apart from my memories, family and friends...

Setúbal is a city in Portugal, located in the Setúbal Peninsula, in the Lisbon metropolitan area. It is also the seat of the Setúbal District and the main city of the Setúbal Municipality. Setúbal was ranked the 11th most livable city in Portugal in 2018.

History

Setúbal was founded in the first millennium BC by the Phoenicians, who created a commercial outpost there. It was then conquered by the Carthaginians, and then by the Romans, who established a fish salting factory there. The Moors conquered the city in the 8th century, and it was then taken by the Portuguese in the 12th century.

Geography

Setúbal is located on the Setúbal Peninsula, which is located between the Sado and Tejo rivers. The city is surrounded by the Arrábida Natural Park, which is known for its beautiful beaches and mountains. The city is also located near the Sado Estuary, which is a protected area that is home to many species of birds and fish.
