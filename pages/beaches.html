Praias

Spending a day by the sea, with your feet soaking in crystal clear turquoise waters, the stillness of a vast ocean and the top of a mountain peeking above your head, is the idyllic image of a beach holiday that seems to only be possible in distant places. . However, this reality is much closer than you think! This natural paradise is called Arrábida and is just 40 minutes from Lisbon. And in this article we will reveal the best beaches in Arrábida, from the trendiest to the wildest.

Praia dos Galapinhos

Praia dos Galapinhos was once considered the best and most beautiful beach in Europe and, with its magnificent landscape and crystal clear waters, it is natural that it impressed those who voted. It's larger than neighboring Praia dos Galapos, but more difficult access means it's a little less crowded. Therefore, it is ideal for those looking for a quieter and more spacious beach, although part of the sand is also occupied by palm trees and sun loungers for rent. During the bathing season there is also lifeguard supervision.
